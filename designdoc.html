08Design principles
01
No radius. Ever.
Zero border-radius is a hard rule. Hard corners communicate authority and raw confidence.
02
Borders are structure.
Every element has a visible border. Borders at 2.5px minimum. Headers and feature elements at 4px.
03
Offset shadow only.
Shadows are hard offset — never blurred. X Y 0. No softness. Shadow makes things feel pressed into the page.
04
Amber is loud.
Use amber surfaces for primary actions only. Don't dilute it. When you see amber, you should know it matters.
05
Mono for body.
All body copy in Space Mono. Monospace feels raw, technical, and honest. Bebas Neue handles all display type.
06
Press = active.
Active/pressed state: element moves 3–5px toward shadow. Shadow disappears. Pure mechanical feedback.
